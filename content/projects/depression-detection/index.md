Dual-degree thesis at IIT Kharagpur under Prof. Adway Mitra. Fine-tuned RoBERTa to classify social media posts into latent psycho-social variables, then modeled causal relationships via a Bayesian network. Combined 18K+ Reddit posts with 900 clinically annotated records. Applied SHAP and LIME for interpretability.
